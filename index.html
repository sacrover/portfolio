Multi Robot Planning Optimization

  • Implemented Discrete Adaptive Particle Swarm Optimzation (DAPSO) to solve the multi-robot task allocation problem for near-optimal task distribution under realistic constraints.
  • Developed a graph-based warehouse model in networkX. This model enabled efficient task allocation and smooth path planning for the robots.
  • Demonstrated a PoC simulation of mulit-robot operation in Gazebo based on the optimization results.
  • Implemented the Regulated Pure Pursuit controller and adjusted its parameters for better lane-following behavior. This allowed the robots to navigate more effectively in the warehouse.
  • Integrated a collision avoidance system alongside junction preference logic. This ensured safe and efficient navigation through complex layouts with minimal conflicts.

Improving Visual SLAM in Motion Blur

  • Developed a pipeline integrating deep deblurring networks with ORB SLAM3 to improve tracking accuracy in visually degraded environments affected by motion blur.
  • Designed and implemented a keypoint filtering algorithm based on blur kernel intensity to enhance feature matching and tracking in blurry frames.
  • Curated a synthetic dataset in Unity3D to simulate various blur intensities for controlled testing of deblurring and tracking performance.
  • Conducted benchmark testing on the EuROC MAV dataset, demonstrating up to 45% improvement in pose tracking accuracy with the proposed deblurring pipeline.
